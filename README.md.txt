# Kori Mrekullia Website

**Kori Mrekullia** is a website created for the Children's Choir of Tirana, "Mrekullia". The platform aims to provide information about the choir, showcase its events and gallery, and allow parents to register their children and support the choir. The website also features a blog, contact forms, event calendar, and donation options.

## Overview
The website was designed with **branding and visual identity** in mind. A professional logo was created for the choir, and the site reflects the choir’s branding colors and style. All pages are structured for an intuitive user experience, with clear navigation, interactive forms, and multimedia content.

---

## Pages Structure
The website consists of 10 main pages, organized for **intuitive navigation**:

1. **Home (index.html)** – Introduction to the choir, hero image, parent testimonials, quick links.  
2. **Join Us (behu-pjese.html)** – Registration form for children with personal data fields, parent contact, and photo upload.  
3. **Blog (blog.html)** – Articles, announcements, and event posts. Email subscription included.  
4. **Contact (kontakt.html)** – Contact form for questions, suggestions, or further information.  
5. **FAQ (faq.html)** – Answers to common questions about participation, events, and payments.  
6. **Gallery (galeria.html)** – Visual presentation of photos and videos from concerts and past events.  
7. **Event Calendar (kalendari.html)** – Dynamic display of concerts, rehearsals, and planned activities.  
8. **About Us (rreth-nesh.html)** – History, vision, mission, and choir leadership team.  
9. **Support (suport.html)** – Donation form for individuals or organizations wishing to support the choir financially.  
10. **Concerts (koncerte.html)** – Information about upcoming and past concerts.

## Interactive Forms
- **Child Registration Form**: Required fields, validation, and photo upload feature. Data is sent securely via email.  
- **Contact & Donation Forms**: User-friendly design; data sent securely to the administrator’s email.

## Blog Subscription
Users can subscribe with their email to receive notifications of new posts automatically.

## Responsive Design
The website uses **responsive design principles** with CSS3 media queries, ensuring proper display on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

## Navigation
- Sticky main menu with direct access to each section.  
- Clear folder organization for files: `pages`, `css`, `js`, `assets` (images, videos).  
- Initially built as static HTML pages, styled with CSS, and enhanced with JavaScript functionality.

- **CSS3** – Styling and responsive design.  
- **JavaScript** – Interactive functionality (forms, gallery, blog subscription).
