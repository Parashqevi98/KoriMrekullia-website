# Kori Mrekullia – Official Website

**Live Website:** https://korimrekullia.al  
*This is a real, fully functional and publicly accessible website, currently in use by the Children's Choir of Tirana.*

---

## Kori Mrekullia Website

**Kori Mrekullia** is a real-world website created for the Children's Choir of Tirana, "Mrekullia".  
The platform is actively used to present the choir’s activities, concerts, and gallery, as well as to allow parents to register their children, contact the choir, and support it financially.

## Overview
The website was designed with a strong focus on **branding, usability, and real-world functionality**.  
A custom logo and visual identity were created specifically for the choir, and the platform reflects its mission, values, and artistic character. The website provides a smooth user experience through clear navigation, responsive layout, and interactive features.

---

## Pages Structure
The website consists of **10 main pages**, organized for intuitive and user-friendly navigation:

1. **Home (index.html)** – Introduction to the choir, hero image, parent testimonials, and quick access links.  
2. **Join Us (behu-pjese.html)** – Child registration form including personal data, parent contact details, and photo upload.  
3. **Blog (blog.html)** – Articles, announcements, and event-related posts with email subscription.  
4. **Contact (kontakt.html)** – Contact form for questions, suggestions, or information requests.  
5. **FAQ (faq.html)** – Frequently asked questions about participation, activities, and payments.  
6. **Gallery (galeria.html)** – Visual presentation of photos from concerts and choir activities.  
7. **Event Calendar (kalendari.html)** – Overview of upcoming concerts, rehearsals, and events.  
8. **About Us (rreth-nesh.html)** – Choir history, vision, mission, and leadership team.  
9. **Support (suport.html)** – Donation form for individuals or organizations supporting the choir.  
10. **Concerts (koncerte.html)** – Dedicated page for past and upcoming concert information.

## Interactive Forms
- **Child Registration Form** with required fields, validation, and file upload functionality.  
- **Contact and Donation Forms** designed for ease of use, sending data securely to the administrator’s email.

Visitors can subscribe using their email address to receive automatic notifications when new blog posts are published.
